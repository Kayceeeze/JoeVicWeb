Dr. Ifeanyichukwu Ezebialu

Medical Director MBBS (Benin), FWACS, FMCOG, Cert SRHR (Geneva)
Dr. Ifeanyichukwu attended the University of Benin where he obtained a medical degree (MBBS) in 2002. He worked briefly as General Practitioner before commencing his Postgraduate training at the Nnamdi Azikiwe University Teaching Hospital, Nnewi. In 2010, he obtained the Fellowship of West African College of Surgeons (FWACS) and in 2011, the Fellowship of the Medical College of Obstetrics and Gynaecology (FMCOG). He is a lecturer in the department of OBGYN, College of Medicine, Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University as well as a clinical consultant in the teaching hospital. He is also the Medical Director of JoeVic Specialist Hospital. He has interest in reproductive and sexual health of women.
